--- title: "Trick or treat!" sub: "We refuse to lose." date: 2009-09-04 12:00:00 image: "story-09.jpg" landscape: true alt: dance marathon trick or treat bags layout: post excerpt_separator: --- In being heartbroken that all the effort in putting together the pumpkin painting event came short on impacting cancer patients, we devised a new strategy: trick or treat bags. It was perfect, it fit the essence of halloween, we didn't need to hand deliver it, and nothing inside could be considered hazardous. The beautiful artwork was done by Anna, and the whole project thoroughly exceeded our expectations.
